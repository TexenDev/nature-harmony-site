1. Information We Do Not Collect

Nature Harmony does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no account registration, no login, and no personal identifiers such as your name, email, IP address, or location are ever requested or processed.

2. App Functionality

To deliver the intended experience, the app uses core device capabilities:

  • Audio Playback: Nature Harmony uses local audio files (e.g., rain, wind, birds) stored within the app to generate relaxing environments.
  • Custom Mixing & Volume Control: Your sound preferences and mixes are stored locally on your device and are never shared externally.
  • Sleep Timer: The timer feature functions entirely on-device and is never synced or transmitted.
  • Background Playback: The app may continue playing sounds in the background if you permit it, even when your screen is locked or you're using other apps.

Nature Harmony does not use or access your microphone, camera, or location.
